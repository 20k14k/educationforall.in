introduction of education for all
Education for All is a coaching center which has been started to help financially weak children in studies. This is also a campaign through which we try to give the best education to the economically weaker children who are not able to pay more fees. The sole purpose of Education for All is that the child who is a little weak financially, this lack of it should not affect his studies.
That's why we say better education in less fees.
